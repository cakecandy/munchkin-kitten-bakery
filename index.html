Introduction

Founded in 2020, the original Munchkin Kitten Bakery ignited a modern spa and treats revolution with ingenious products, a super-positive, never-fussy vibe, and thin mints. Did we mention the thin mints?

We want everyone to give their kitten the best. So, we leverage the newest technologies (and crunch so many numbers) to bring you treat and spa services that work in every budget.

About Us

At Munchkin Kitten Bakery, we believe there is a better way to do marketing. A more valuable, less invasive way where customers are earned rather than bought. We're obsessively passionate about it, and our mission is to help people achieve it. We focus on treat & spa optimization.
